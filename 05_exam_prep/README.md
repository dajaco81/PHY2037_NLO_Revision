## Exam Format

- **70% written exam** in May (past papers with solutions on ELE)
- **30% group video assessment** due Friday Week 11
- Revision sessions offered in May before the exam

## Key Facts

- Equations given on exam front sheet: Fourier transform of Gaussian, standard integrals used in phase matching derivation, sinc function relation
- Equations NOT given (must memorise): pulse energy/power/intensity formulas, time-bandwidth product (0.441 for Gaussian), FWHM/σ conversion, atomic field strength relation
